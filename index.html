Get My Offer

How do you determine your offer price?

We pull recent comparable sales in your exact submarket, assess repair scope with a licensed GC, subtract holding costs and a target margin, and write the offer.

Do you charge any fees or commissions?

Never. No commissions, no closing costs, no junk fees. The offer you see is the number that hits your bank account.

What if the house is in really bad shape?

That's our specialty. Water damage, structural issues, hoarding, fire — we underwrite with those in mind. Don't clean, don't repair, don't hide anything.

How fast can you actually close?

As fast as title can clear — often 7 days for a clean title, up to 21 when probate or lienholders are involved. Need more time? We'll accommodate up to 90 days at no extra cost.
